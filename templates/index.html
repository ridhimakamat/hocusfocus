How it works

Effortless tracking,
from day one

No forms to fill. No ratings to give. Just open HocusFocus, start working, and let it do the rest.

Step 01
Name your task
Type what you're working on and choose screen or offline mode. That's the only input HocusFocus ever needs from you.
Step 02
Webcam monitors quietly
HocusFocus watches your presence in the background. No data leaves your device — completely private, always local.
Step 03
Gesture your breaks
Hold up fingers to take a break — the number you show becomes the minutes you get. No buttons, no timers to set.
Step 04
See your real numbers
At session end, discover your true effective time, what cost you focus, and how your productivity trends over weeks.
Features

Everything you need
to work at your best

Six thoughtfully designed systems that work together, invisibly, in the background.

Core feature

Automatic effective time

HocusFocus calculates your true productive minutes by subtracting every absence and break from your total session time. What remains is what you actually achieved — no guesswork, no self-reporting, just honest measurement.

Presence

Absence detection

HocusFocus knows when you step away from your desk. Absence time is excluded automatically — your effective minutes only count time you were genuinely present and focused.

Flexibility

Screen and offline modes

Whether you are coding on a laptop or reading from a textbook, choose the mode that fits. HocusFocus adapts its tracking to how you are actually working today.

Break system

Gesture-triggered breaks

Hold up fingers to the camera to take a break — the number shown becomes the break duration in minutes. No buttons, no timers to set. Breaks are logged separately and excluded from effective time.

Insights

Session analytics

Charts show your planned versus effective time, strongest focus days, break patterns, and productivity trends — generated automatically from your session history.

Break system

Your hand is the
only remote you need

Hold fingers up to the camera and HocusFocus automatically pauses your session — the count becomes your break in minutes. Use one hand for 1–5 minutes, both hands for 6–10 minutes.

3 fingers → 3 min break
Live gesture preview

Simply hold your hand up to the webcam. HocusFocus reads how many fingers you're showing and immediately starts a break timer for that many minutes. When the break ends, your session resumes automatically.

No keyboard. No mouse. No interruption. The break is logged separately and excluded from your effective time — so your productivity data stays honest.

Show a closed fist and HocusFocus knows you're staying focused. The gesture system is always watching, always ready, and never gets in your way.
